Basic Math

Addition

Finding the total, or sum, by combining two or more numbers. Example: 5 + 11 + 3 = 19 is an addition.

Subtraction

Subtraction in mathematics means you are taking something away from a group or number of things. An example of a subtraction problem is the following: 5 - 3 = 2.
